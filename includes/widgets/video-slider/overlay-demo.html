How to Use in Elementor

  1. Add the Video Slider widget to your page
  2. Go to the Style tab
  3. Find the Background Overlay section
  4. Enable the overlay with the toggle switch
  5. Choose your overlay color
  6. Adjust the opacity (0 = transparent, 1 = opaque)
  7. Optionally select a blend mode for creative effects

Benefits

  • Better Text Readability: Dark overlays improve text contrast over bright images/videos
  • Brand Consistency: Apply your brand colors as overlays
  • Visual Hierarchy: Use overlays to direct attention to specific slides
  • Creative Effects: Blend modes create unique visual styles
  • Accessibility: Improved contrast ratios for better accessibility
